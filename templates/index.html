Temperature

/api/v1.0/tobs

JSON of the minimum, average, and maximum temperature for the last year in the dataset. click here

Temperature Range

/api/v1.0/(start)

/api/v1.0/(start)/(end)

JSON of the minimum, average, and maximum temperature for a certain date range. Dates are passed in YYYY-MM-DD format. Dates range from 2010-01-01 to 2017-08-23. Start endpoint gives data past that date (inclusive) and start/end gives data within that range (inclusive). Link coming soon.
